What is Coaching?

Provides aspiring cricketers with world-class training and best in class infrastructure to help them reach the next level. Our cricket program focuses on specialized coaching for batting, bowling, and wicket-keeping. The amount of training is assigned as per the skills and fitness level of the student, and adequate measures are taken care of to ensure the sport is fun for the beginners.

The Team 3S

The Team 3S is associated with Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), for grades 5 to 10th, which is one of Indias premier educational bodies offering qualifications for more than 21,000 schools in over 28 countries over the world. For grades 11th and 12th, we are associated with the Karnataka State Board. For University, Graduate & Post Graduate courses, the Sports School offers courses by Jain Group Of Institutions (JGI) who are our educational partners and continually ranks among the top universities in India.

The Team 3S cricket academy is equipped with new-age facilities that aid the students to elevate their everyday life and give a comfortable space to pursue their passion.

  • Weekly practice matches for exposure
  • Inter-school matches U-14, U-16
  • KSCA Inter-school matches U-14, U-16
  • KSCA league matches U-14, U-16, U-19, Seniors

The values of excellence, integrity, academic freedom, and accountability are central to our mission of balancing academics into our sport syllabi.

BATTING

In cricket, batting is the act or skill of hitting the ball with a bat to score runs and prevent the loss of one's wicket.

BOWLING

Bowling, in cricket, is the action of propelling the ball toward the wicket defended by a batter.

FIELDING

Fielding refers to the act of catching, collecting and returning a ball that has been hit by the batsman.

WICKET-KEEPING

The wicket-keeper in the sport of cricket is the player on the fielding side who stands behind the wicket

COACHING

The Cricket Coaching foundation skills and insights are based upon the brain based model

UMPIRING

In cricket, an umpire is to make decisions about events on the cricket field according to the Laws of Cricket.
